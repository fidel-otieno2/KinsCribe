# How to View & Manage Archived and Highlighted Stories

## 📦 Archived Stories

### Where to Find Them
1. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation)
2. Tap the **Archive tab** (📦 icon)
3. All your archived stories appear in a grid

### How to View & Unarchive
1. **Tap any archived story** thumbnail
2. Full-screen viewer opens showing:
   - Story media (image/video)
   - Title and content
   - **"Unarchive Story" button** (orange)
3. Tap **"Unarchive Story"** to restore it to Family Feed
4. Story immediately returns to family feed and disappears from archive

### How to Archive a Story
1. Go to **Family** screen
2. Find your story in the feed
3. Tap the **📦 Archive icon** in the action row
4. Story is hidden from family feed and moved to your archive

---

## ⭐ Highlighted Stories

### Where to Find Them
1. Go to **Family** screen
2. Scroll to **"Family Highlights"** section at top of feed
3. Horizontal scrollable cards show all highlighted stories

### How to View & Remove Highlight
1. **Tap any highlight card**
2. Full-screen viewer opens showing:
   - Story media (image/video)
   - Title and content
   - **"Remove Highlight" button** (if you're owner or admin)
3. Tap **"Remove Highlight"** to remove from highlights section
4. Story remains in family feed but no longer featured in highlights

### How to Highlight a Story
1. Go to **Family** screen
2. Find a story in the feed
3. Tap the **⭐ Star icon** in the action row
4. Story appears in "Family Highlights" section with a purple badge

---

## 🎯 Quick Reference

### Archive (Owner Only)
- **Archive**: Tap 📦 icon on your story → Hidden from feed
- **View**: Profile → Archive tab → Tap story
- **Unarchive**: Open archived story → Tap "Unarchive Story" → Back to feed

### Highlight (Owner or Admin)
- **Highlight**: Tap ⭐ icon on any story → Featured at top
- **View**: Family → "Family Highlights" section → Tap card
- **Remove**: Open highlighted story → Tap "Remove Highlight" → No longer featured

---

## 💡 Tips

1. **Archived stories are private** - Only you can see them in your archive
2. **Highlighted stories are public** - Everyone in the family sees them featured
3. **Archive to declutter** - Hide old stories without deleting them
4. **Highlight important moments** - Feature special memories at the top of the feed
5. **Admins can highlight any story** - Family admins can feature any member's story
6. **Stories keep their data** - Likes, comments, and content are preserved when archived/highlighted

---

## 🔄 Story Lifecycle

```
Create Story
    ↓
Family Feed (visible to all)
    ↓
[Archive] → Your Archive (private) → [Unarchive] → Back to Feed
    ↓
[Highlight] → Family Highlights (featured) → [Remove] → Still in Feed
```

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I archive someone else's story?**
A: No, only the story owner can archive their own stories.

**Q: Can I highlight someone else's story?**
A: Yes, if you're a family admin. Story owners can always highlight their own stories.

**Q: What happens to likes/comments when I archive?**
A: They're preserved. When you unarchive, all likes and comments return.

**Q: Can others see my archived stories?**
A: No, archived stories are completely private and only visible in your profile archive.

**Q: How many stories can I highlight?**
A: No limit. All highlighted stories appear in the Family Highlights section.

**Q: Can I archive and highlight the same story?**
A: No, archived stories are hidden from the feed, so they can't be highlighted.
